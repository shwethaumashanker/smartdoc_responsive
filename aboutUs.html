Why was Smart Doctors born?
One morning I woke up with fever, coughing non-stop, and also feeling shortness of breath. I had to call my friend who happened to be in the apartment to help me get up from the bed as I could barely stand up on my own. A fear of contracting the deadly coronavirus suddenly came into my mind especially at this sensitive time. But what also worried me was that the hospitals could be packed with patients already for the spread of the virus and it would be an endless wait for me to get to be treated.
And then as a techie like me, an idea came into my mind… “Why can’t we just bring the medical resources home?” “Wouldn’t it be great for a group of people putting their hearts into saving the world and battling the deadly COVID-19 with a group of licensed medical practitioners treating patients virtually?”
Not only will the patients avoid the hassle by waiting forever to see the doctor with the risk of catching the virus in the hospital, but it will also protect the doctors and nurses from being in close contact with the patients and getting infected.
